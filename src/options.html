Autofill Okta - Options

Autofill Okta requires a Pushbullet Access Token to function. To
acquire a Pushbullet Access Token, install Pushbullet on your phone,
then visit your account settings page:
https://www.pushbullet.com/#settings
Scroll down until you see the "Create Access Token" button, click it
then copy & paste the access token into here, then click "Save".
